Your Week Starts Here

Bruges is full of things to discover...

Fresh waffles, incredible bakeries, artisan chocolate, hidden cafés and restaurants we'll still be talking about long after we get home.

This week is about following your curiosity—and your appetite.

Your Role

Nobody spots good food quite like you.

Whether it's a bakery tucked down a side street, an irresistible dessert menu or somewhere serving the perfect lunch, you'll help us discover the places we'd otherwise have walked straight past.

Every great trip deserves great meals.

Your Mission

  • Find the best bakery in Bruges.
  • Choose a dessert worth sharing.
  • Discover one hidden café.
  • Convince someone to try something new.

Our Week

Monday – First Taste

We'll arrive, enjoy our first Belgian beer overlooking the Markt and settle in with dinner together at Mozart.

Tuesday – Taste & Explore

Walking tour, shopping and plenty of opportunities to sample Bruges one coffee, chocolate or pastry at a time.

Wednesday – Discover

A little competition in the escape room before another afternoon of wandering, snacking and seeing what catches our eye.

Thursday – A Feast

The luxury day. Relaxed surroundings, wonderful food and a fantastic Argentinian steak to round off the evening.

Friday – One Last Treat

Before we leave, there's always time for one last coffee, one last pastry and one last stroll through Bruges.

Your Objective

Return home having found...

the best thing we ate all week.
