Welcome, Michelle

Keeper of the Pace

Your Week Starts Here

This week comes with no expectations.

No alarms. No schedules to chase. No pressure to do everything.

Just beautiful streets, good food, laughter and the chance to enjoy every moment at your own pace.

Your Role

Every trip needs someone who reminds everyone else that slowing down is never wasted time.

Whether it's another coffee, another look around a little shop or simply sitting together watching the world go by, those moments often become the ones we remember most.

If we're enjoying ourselves, we're exactly where we're supposed to be.

Your Mission

  • Find the prettiest place to stop for coffee.
  • Encourage at least one unplanned detour.
  • Take time to enjoy the little moments.
  • Make sure nobody feels rushed.

Our Week

Monday – Arrive & Relax

Our holiday begins with a drink overlooking the Markt before dinner together at Mozart. A gentle evening to settle into Bruges.

Tuesday – Wander

A relaxed walking tour followed by cafés, shops and discovering Bruges without watching the clock.

Wednesday – Little Adventures

The escape room brings a bit of fun before another afternoon of wandering wherever looks inviting.

Thursday – Luxury Day

Slow breakfast, a drink in the gardens at Dukes' Palace and a wonderful evening meal together.

Friday – One Last Look

A final stroll through Bruges before heading home with plenty of memories to take with us.

Your Objective

Come home feeling...

completely recharged.
