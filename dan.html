Welcome

For a few days, life slows down. No rushing. No pressure. Just great company, good food, Belgian beer and the freedom to wander wherever Bruges takes us.

The Bruges Philosophy

This isn't a holiday about seeing everything. It's about finding the little moments that become the stories we tell afterwards. We'll happily abandon the plan if something even better appears.

The only rule: If something better than the plan appears... that's the new plan.

Chief Curator

Your Mission

Quietly create five days everyone remembers. Relax and let Bruges do the rest.

Our Week

Monday — Arrival

We'll settle into Bruges, enjoy our first Belgian beer together at Duvelorium, then head to Mozart for dinner.

Tuesday — Explore

11:00 walking tour followed by shopping, cafés, Belgian beer and hopefully ODA Rooftop Bar.

Wednesday — Adventure

Escape room, wandering Bruges and hopefully live music somewhere special.

Thursday — Indulgence

Breakfast at Carpe Diem, drinks at Dukes' Palace and an Argentinian steak.

Friday — Until Next Time

Breakfast, one last wander, shopping and the journey home.
