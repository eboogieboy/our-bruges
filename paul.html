Welcome, Paul

A Week to Slow Down

Some people collect souvenirs. Others collect stories worth telling over a pint.

The holiday doesn't begin when we cross into Belgium.

It begins on Sunday evening, when everyone's together, the bags are packed and tomorrow's only job is getting to Bruges.

From that point on, there's nowhere to rush, nowhere you absolutely have to be and no decisions that really matter.

Just good company, Belgian beer and the freedom to stay a little longer whenever somewhere feels worth it.

We've Got a Feeling...

Before the week is over, you'll probably have settled at least one debate, discovered a beer worth recommending and declared one restaurant the clear winner.

You'll also almost certainly convince everyone to stay for “just one more”.

We wouldn't expect anything less.

Your Mission

  • Find the best Belgian beer.
  • Find the longest tasting stick.
  • Keep the group moving whenever a decision needs making.
  • Officially declare when we've eaten the best meal of the trip.

Your Week of Good Decisions

Monday – A Strong Start

Belgian beer on the Duvelorium balcony, drinks at ODA Rooftop and ribs at Mozart. Not a bad first day.

Tuesday – Follow Your Instincts

A Taste of Bruges walking tour at 11:30am, followed by cafés, shops and somewhere worth stopping for a beer.

Wednesday – Trust Your Gut

Escape the escape room, then let the evening make its own plans.

Thursday – Save Room

A peaceful afternoon in the Dukes' Palace gardens before what might become the best steak of the trip.

Friday – One More Before We Go

A final breakfast, one last wander and perhaps one more Belgian beer before heading home.

Your Objective

Return home knowing exactly

where everyone should go next time.
