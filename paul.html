Welcome, Paul

This week isn't about rushing around.

It's about finding great beer, eating great food and enjoying one of Europe's best cities with good friends.

Take your time. Bruges isn't going anywhere.

Your Role

Decision Maker

When the group can't decide... you probably will.

Left or right. One more beer. Another steak. Stay here a bit longer.

Somebody has to make the difficult decisions.

Your Mission

  • Find the best Belgian beer of the trip.
  • Order an incredible steak.
  • Discover one pub we'll all want to revisit.
  • Never let anyone rush a good conversation.

Our Week

Monday – Arrival

The holiday begins with our first Belgian beer at Duvelorium before dinner together at Mozart. Settle in, slow down and enjoy the start of the week.

Tuesday – Explore

A relaxed day discovering Bruges. We'll enjoy the walking tour, browse the shops, stop for good coffee, and hopefully finish with a drink overlooking the rooftops.

Wednesday – Adventure

Time for a little friendly competition in the escape room, followed by wandering wherever looks interesting. If we stumble across live music, even better.

Thursday – Treat Yourself

Today's all about taking it easy. A leisurely breakfast, a relaxing drink in the gardens at Dukes' Palace and an outstanding Argentinian steak to finish the evening.

Friday – One Last Wander

Enjoy one final coffee, one last Belgian beer if time allows, and soak up the atmosphere before heading home.

Your objective:

Return home having found
the best beer of the week.
