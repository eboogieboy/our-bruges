Welcome, Paul

Decision Maker

A Week to Slow Down

This week isn't about rushing around.

It's about finding great beer, eating great food and enjoying one of Europe's most beautiful cities with good friends.

Take your time. Bruges isn't going anywhere.

Your Role

When the group can't decide, you probably will.

Left or right. One more beer. Another steak. Stay here a little longer.

Your Mission

  • Find the best Belgian beer.
  • Find the longest tasting stick.
  • Keep the group moving whenever a decision needs making.
  • Officially declare when we've eaten the best meal of the trip.

Our Week

Monday – Start Properly

Belgian beer on the Duvelorium balcony, drinks at ODA Rooftop and ribs at Mozart.

Tuesday – Find the Good Stuff

A Taste of Bruges walking tour at 11:30am, followed by cafés, shops and somewhere worth stopping for a beer.

Wednesday – Make the Call

Escape room, a few decisions for the group and an evening wherever has the best atmosphere.

Thursday – Steak Night

A relaxed day, drinks in the Dukes' Palace gardens and the trip's big Argentinian steak dinner.

Friday – One Last Round

Breakfast, a final wander and perhaps one last Belgian beer before heading home.

Your Objective

Return home having discovered

the best Belgian beer of the week.
