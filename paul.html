Welcome, Paul

Decision Maker

A Week to Slow Down

This week isn't about rushing around.

It's about finding great beer, eating great food and enjoying one of Europe's most beautiful cities with good friends.

Take your time. Bruges isn't going anywhere.

Your Role

When the group can't decide... you probably will.

Left or right. One more beer. Another steak. Stay here a little longer.

Somebody has to make the difficult decisions.

Your Mission

  • Find the best Belgian beer of the trip.
  • Order an unforgettable steak.
  • Discover one pub we'll all recommend.
  • Never rush a good conversation.

Our Week

Monday – Arrival

Our first Belgian beer at Duvelorium before dinner together at Mozart. A relaxed evening to settle into Bruges.

Tuesday – Explore

A gentle day discovering Bruges. Walking tour, cafés, shops and hopefully a drink overlooking the rooftops.

Wednesday – Adventure

Escape room, wandering wherever looks interesting and maybe finding some live music in the evening.

Thursday – Treat Yourself

A slower day with breakfast, drinks in the gardens at Dukes' Palace and an outstanding Argentinian steak.

Friday – One Last Wander

Enjoy one last coffee, one final Belgian beer if time allows and soak up the atmosphere before heading home.

Your Objective

Return home having discovered...

the best Belgian beer of the week.
