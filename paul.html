A Week to Slow Down

This week isn't about rushing around.

It's about finding great beer, eating great food and enjoying one of Europe's most beautiful cities with good friends.

Take your time. Bruges isn't going anywhere.

Your Role

When the group can't decide, you probably will.

Left or right. One more beer. Another steak. Stay here a little longer.

Your Mission

  • Find the best Belgian beer.
  • Order an unforgettable steak.
  • Discover one pub everyone recommends.
  • Never rush a good conversation.

Our Week

Monday – Arrival

Duvelorium, Mozart and a relaxed first evening.

Tuesday – Explore

Walking tour, cafés, shops and a rooftop drink if the weather plays along.

Wednesday – Adventure

Escape room and wandering wherever looks interesting.

Thursday – Treat Yourself

Dukes' Palace gardens and an outstanding Argentinian steak.

Friday – Home

One final coffee, one last wander and home.

Your Objective

Return home having discovered

the best Belgian beer of the week.
