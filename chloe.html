Welcome, Chloe

Memory Maker

Your Week Starts Here

Bruges is the perfect place for a little bit of chaos, plenty of laughter and the kind of moments nobody could have planned.

Beautiful streets, great food, good drinks and five days with people who are ready to enjoy themselves.

Your job is to make sure the trip never becomes too sensible.

Your Role

Every holiday needs someone who brings the energy.

The unexpected photo. The spontaneous detour. The ridiculous conversation that somehow becomes the story everyone tells when they get home.

You help turn a good trip into a memorable one.

Your Mission

  • Create the funniest photo of the trip.
  • Start one completely unplanned adventure.
  • Find somewhere with a brilliant atmosphere.
  • Make sure everyone laughs at least once every day.

Our Week

Monday – Let It Begin

We arrive, settle in and start properly with a drink overlooking the Markt before dinner together at Mozart.

Tuesday – Explore

A walking tour, shops, cafés and plenty of chances to find something interesting, funny or completely unexpected.

Wednesday – Game On

Escape room, wandering Bruges and hopefully finding some live music or somewhere with a great atmosphere.

Thursday – Dress Up & Enjoy

A slower luxury day with beautiful surroundings, drinks at Dukes' Palace and a great steak dinner together.

Friday – Final Memories

One last wander, one last group photo and a final chance to add another story before we head home.

Your Objective

Make sure we return home with...

at least one story we cannot tell everyone.
