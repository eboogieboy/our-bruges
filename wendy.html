Your Week Starts Here

Bruges isn't a city to rush.

It's a place to wander, stop whenever somewhere catches your eye, and enjoy long conversations over coffee, wine and great food.

This week is about making time for those moments.

Your Role

Every group has someone who instinctively finds the perfect place to stop.

A quiet table beside the canal. A hidden courtyard. A sunny terrace. A cosy restaurant tucked down a side street.

You have a talent for finding places that make everyone want to stay just a little longer.

Your Mission

  • Find the prettiest place for coffee.
  • Choose the best outdoor table.
  • Discover one beautiful street nobody expected.
  • Make sure we pause often enough to enjoy the city.

Our Week

Monday – Arrive & Unwind

We'll ease into Bruges with our first drink overlooking the Markt before dinner together at Mozart. No rushing—just enjoying the start of the holiday.

Tuesday – Explore

A relaxed walking tour followed by plenty of time to browse shops, discover cafés and enjoy wherever the streets lead us.

Wednesday – Discover

A little adventure with the escape room before another afternoon of wandering and seeing what catches our eye.

Thursday – Indulge

Our luxury day. A leisurely breakfast, drinks at Dukes' Palace Garden Bar and a wonderful evening meal together.

Friday – Savour the Last Morning

One last stroll through Bruges before heading home with lots of happy memories.

Your Objective

Find the place...

that none of us will ever forget.
